About me

comedian and actor

Mr. Bean is a fictional character created and portrayed by the British actor and comedian Rowan Atkinson. The character first appeared in a television series titled "Mr. Bean," which ran from 1990 to 1995. Mr. Bean is known for his comically absurd and often silent antics, navigating through everyday life with a childlike and mischievous demeanor.
